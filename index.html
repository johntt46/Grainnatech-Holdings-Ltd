About Grainnatech

Our Mission

Grainnatech Holdings Ltd develops sovereign cognitive infrastructure — systems that allow organisations to operate with full autonomy over their decision-making processes, data governance, and institutional memory. We believe that the foundations of organisational intelligence should be owned, auditable, and resilient.

Our Origin

Founded in Tuam, County Galway, Ireland, Grainnatech draws from a tradition of careful stewardship and long-horizon thinking. Our name reflects a commitment to grain-level precision in the technology we build — infrastructure that is both robust at scale and faithful in detail.

What We Build

Our platform provides cognitive runners, governance engines, compliance frameworks, and audit trail systems that operate as a unified whole. Every component is designed for offline-first resilience, deterministic execution, and full sovereignty — no external dependencies required.

Who We Serve

We serve institutions, regulatory bodies, holding companies, and any organisation that requires provable, auditable governance. Our systems are built for environments where trust must be earned through evidence, not assumed through reputation.

Governance Framework

Provable compliance. Auditable decisions. Institutional memory that endures.

Evidence-Based Compliance

Every governance decision produces a cryptographically anchored evidence record. Compliance is not declared — it is proven through an immutable chain of evidence entries that can be independently verified at any time.

Council Structure

Governance operates through defined councils with clear mandates, membership rules, and decision protocols. The system enforces separation of concerns while enabling coordinated action across the full organisational hierarchy.

Policy Propagation

Policies defined at holding level propagate through subsidiaries with configurable inheritance. Local overrides are permitted within defined bounds, ensuring both consistency and operational flexibility.

Audit Trail

Complete, tamper-evident audit logs capture every state transition, policy evaluation, and governance action. The trail is designed for regulatory inspection and long-term archival integrity.

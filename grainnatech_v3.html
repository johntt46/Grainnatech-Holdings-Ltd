A New Industry Defined

Grainnatech Holdings Ltd designs, builds, and governs sovereign cognitive infrastructure — technology systems that are constitutionally governed, cryptographically verifiable, and human-centred. Founded in Tuam, County Galway, Ireland, Grainnatech defines a new industry: constitutionally governed, human-centred, lease-only, evidence-first technology that cannot be acquired.

Governance is not an add-on — it is the foundation. Every system is deterministic, auditable, human-overseen, and ethically bound. The fusion of governance and engineering produces infrastructure that operates across government, defence (non-weapon), enterprise, European institutional, healthcare, financial, and research sectors.
